About This Platform

A collaborative effort to democratize AI safety education

The Mission

This platform was collaboratively built as a free resource for anyone wishing to learn more about technical AI safety or take their first steps into this crucial field. I believe that understanding how AI systems work is essential for ensuring they remain beneficial and aligned with human values.

How It Was Built

This interactive learning platform was created through a unique collaboration between human and AI. The technical content, code examples, and explanations were generated by Anthropic's Claude AI assistant with human oversight, curation, and direction. This collaboration demonstrates how AI can be used to accelerate education and lower barriers to important technical knowledge.

Attribution & Inspiration

The transformer lessons draw substantial inspiration from ARENA (AI Alignment Research Engineers Accelerator), specifically their excellent Lesson 1.1 on building a transformer from scratch. ARENA's comprehensive curriculum has been invaluable in shaping the approach of how to teach these concepts effectively.

Learners who complete InterpSchool's interactive exercises are encouraged to explore ARENA's full curriculum for deeper study and more advanced topics.

Features

  • Interactive Coding Exercises: Multiple exercise types including guided (mcq), challenge (fill-in-the-blank), and freestyle (write from scratch)
  • Learn by Doing: Concepts and implementation are integrated - understand an idea, then code it immediately
  • AI Safety Focus: Every lesson connects technical concepts to AI safety implications
  • No Setup Required: Works directly in the browser

Learning Modes

Each exercise offers three modes to match your learning style:

  • Guided (MCQ): Select the correct answer from multiple choices - great for beginners or quick review
  • Challenge: Fill in the blanks (___) in code templates - test your recall without starting from scratch
  • Freestyle: Write the code from scratch using only hints - the ultimate test of understanding

Switch between modes anytime using the toggle at the top of each exercise!
